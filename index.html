About InterAlyze

InterAlyze is a novel AI-driven job interview analytics platform that offers recruiters insights into the performance of candidates by analyzing verbal and non-verbal cues. The tool analyses online interview videos to gauge key behavioral benchmarks such as self-confidence level, personality traits, and attention metrics. InterAlyze uses NLP, computer vision, and audio processing to deliver an objective viewpoint to aid recruitment. By enhancing candidate evaluation, InterAlyze drastically reduces hiring time, minimizes human bias, and ensures companies select the best-fit talent.
