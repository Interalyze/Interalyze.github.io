About Interalyze

Interalyze provides recruiters with data-driven insights into candidate performance by analyzing both verbal and non-verbal cues.

Features

  • Speech-to-Text: Converts speech to text for analysis.
  • Text Analysis: Stress levels, personality, and skill extraction.
  • Visual Cues: Tracks gaze, expressions, and engagement.
  • Reports: Combines analysis into detailed candidate reports.
