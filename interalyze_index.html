About Interalyze

Interalyze is an interview analytics platform that provides recruiters with data-driven insights into candidate performance by analyzing both verbal and non-verbal cues.

How It Works

  1. Speech-to-Text: Converts speech to text for NLP analysis.
  2. Text Analysis:
    • Stress Levels based on speech patterns.
    • Big 5 Personality analysis.
    • Skills Extraction.
  3. Visual Cues:
    • Attention Metrics by tracking eye gaze and head movement.
    • Confidence Analysis from facial expressions.
  4. Comprehensive Report: Combines NLP results and computer vision analysis.

Features

  • Verbal Analysis: Stress markers, personality, and skill extraction.
  • Non-Verbal Analysis: Eye movements, facial expressions, and head position.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Clear metrics to aid recruiters.
